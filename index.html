Introducing Sum Slam

A fast-paced math game that challenges your calculation skills and quick thinking

Sum Slam is an addictive math game where you solve equations by swiping up or down toward the correct answer. Race against a timer that gets shorter as you advance, and see if you can beat your high score!

  • Challenge yourself with addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division
  • Intuitive swipe controls - just swipe toward the answer
  • Race against a countdown timer that decreases with each level
  • Track your high score and compete on global leaderboards
